% Air, Bat, Bird, Bug, Dark, Dragon, Electric, Fairy, Fighting, Fire, Fish, Ghost, Grass, Ground, Ice, Light, Normal, Poison, Psychic, Rock, Steel, Water

\begin{itemize}
\item new types: Air, Bat, Bird, Fish, Light
\item deprecated: Flying
\item Air:
\subitem Air is super effective vs Bat
\subitem Air is not very effective vs Bug, Dragon, Rock
\subitem Electric is not very effective vs Air
\item Bat:
\subitem Bat is super effective vs Dark, Fighting, Psychic
\subitem Bat is not very effective vs Ghost, Rock
\subitem Bat has no effect vs Steel
\subitem Air, Ice, Light, Rock are super effective vs Bat
\subitem Ghost is not very effective vs Bat
\subitem Ground has no effect vs Bat
\item Bird:
\subitem Bird is super effective vs Bug, Fighting
\subitem Bird is not very effective vs Electric, Rock, Steel
\subitem Electric, Rock are super effective vs Bird
\subitem Bug, Fighting, Grass are not very effective vs Bird
\subitem Ground has no effect vs Bird
\item Fish:
\subitem Fish is not very effective vs Ice, Rock, Steel
\subitem Water is not very effective vs Fish
\item Light:
\subitem Light is super effective vs Bat, Dark, Ghost
\subitem Light is not very effective vs Fire, Grass, Steel
\subitem Dark, Fairy, Ghost are not very effective vs Light
\item other changes:
\subitem Electric is normally effective vs Dragon % instead of not very
\subitem Electric is super effective vs Steel (and vice versa) % instead of normal
\subitem Fairy is normally effective vs Fire % instead of not very
\subitem Fairy is not very effective vs Grass % instead of normal
\subitem Fairy has no effect vs Psychic % instead of normal
\subitem Ground is not very effective vs Dragon % instead of normal
\subitem Ice is super effective vs Fire (and vice versa) % instead of not very
\subitem Ice is normally effective vs Ground % instead of super
\subitem Ice is normally effective vs Steel % instead of not very
\subitem Poison is not very effective vs Steel % instead of no effect
\subitem Psychic is not very effective vs Bug % instead of normal
\subitem Psychic is normally effective vs Steel % instead of not very
\subitem Steel is super effective vs Electric (and vice versa) % instead of not very
\subitem Steel is not very effective vs Fighting % instead of normal
\subitem Steel is normally effective vs Fire % instead of not very
\subitem Steel is normally effective vs Ice % instead of super
\subitem Steel is normally effective vs Rock % instead of super
\end{itemize}
